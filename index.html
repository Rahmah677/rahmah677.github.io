Bioscience Calculus: Oil Palm Growth

The Scenario: Fruit Bunch Maturation

In Malaysia's food security context, predicting the Rate of Change in crop biomass is vital. This graph models the growth of a Fresh Fruit Bunch (FFB) over a 20-week maturation cycle.

Growth Function (Logistic): $$f(x) = \frac{L}{1 + e^{-k(x-x_0)}}$$ Rate of Change (Derivative): $$f'(x) = k \cdot f(x) \cdot \left(1 - \frac{f(x)}{L}\right)$$

Parameters based on realistic MPOB (Malaysian Palm Oil Board) growth curves: $L=25kg, k=0.5, x_0=10$.
